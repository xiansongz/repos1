What is Tyler Coin?

Decentralized

Tyler Coin is an open source proof-of-steak (yum) decentralized peer-to-peer ERC-20 token based on ethereum

Really Fast

Since Tyler Coin runs on the ethereum network, it is super fast; almost as fast as some other popular crypto currencies.. such as ethereum!

Fee-less

One of the best parts is that Tyler Coin is completely fee-less. Since it is currently running on the testnet and doesn't use real eth, we can technically say it is fee-less.

Stable

One of the great benefits to no one using your coin, is that the price remains at a very stable $0.
